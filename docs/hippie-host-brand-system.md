# Hippie Host Brand System

## Core Positioning
Hippie Host is premium boutique web hosting with good vibes, real support, and freedom from soulless corporate hosting.

## Brand Promise
We keep your website up, your email working, and your stress level down.

## Personality
- welcoming
- calm
- clever
- trustworthy
- independent
- slightly rebellious
- premium but approachable
- smart, not nerdy

## Ideal Customers
- small businesses
- creatives
- local companies
- entrepreneurs
- agencies
- people tired of bad support
- people who want real help

## Visual Direction
Create an upscale boutique hippie-inspired hosting brand. The design should feel like modern SaaS polish mixed with warm 1970s freedom aesthetics. It should be memorable, calm, premium, and human - not goofy, cartoonish, or cheap.

## Logo Direction
Preferred logo style:
- wordmark: HIPPIE HOST
- clean symbol/icon
- minimal premium retro style
- flat line art, not cartoon clipart

Icon concepts:
- peace sign + WiFi signal blend
- vintage van + cloud line art
- flower with server nodes center
- smiling sun + hosting cloud
- retro badge emblem

## Color Palette
Primary:
- Forest Green: #23412F
- Sage: #7D9A7A
- Cream: #F5F1E8
- Sand: #D8C5A2
- Charcoal: #2B2B2B

Accent:
- Sunset Orange: #D97A43
- Mustard Gold: #C89C2B

## Typography
Headings:
- Inter, Sora, Outfit, or Manrope
- bold, modern, readable

Body:
- clean sans-serif
- high readability

Logo/accent:
- subtle retro rounded treatment if available
- do not use novelty fonts sitewide

## Homepage Mood
- modern SaaS polish
- boutique lifestyle warmth
- subtle 70s freedom influence
- premium hosting trust
- rounded cards
- soft shadows
- earthy gradients
- subtle grain/texture if practical
- nature-inspired curves
- trust-building hosting visuals

## Imagery
Use:
- tasteful retro travel/nature influence
- mountains, sunrise, road, calm sky
- abstract hosting dashboards
- server/cloud/email/security visuals
- subtle vintage van or peace references

Avoid:
- stoner stereotypes
- tie-dye overload
- cheap cartoon hippie art
- messy vintage clutter
- generic corporate call-center stock imagery

## Brand Voice
Helpful expert friend. Clear, human, direct, lightly playful.

Avoid:
- corporate buzzword nonsense
- fake hype
- excessive slang
- parody hippie language

Good copy style:
"We keep your website up, your email working, and your stress level down."

Bad copy style:
"Synergize your scalable cloud future."

## Taglines
Preferred:
- Web Hosting With Good Vibes
- Hosting That Feels Human Again
- Good Vibes. Great Uptime.
- Freedom From Bad Hosting
- Peace, Love & Reliable Hosting

## Primary Homepage Headline
Web Hosting With Good Vibes

## Homepage Subheadline
Fast, secure, human-supported hosting for websites, email, and small businesses that want peace of mind.

## Product / Plan Names
- Peaceful Start
- Groovy Business
- Cosmic Care
- Freedom Pro, future advanced plan

## Recommended Pricing
Peaceful Start - $14.99/mo
Groovy Business - $29.99/mo - Most Popular
Cosmic Care - $59.99/mo

## AI Assistant Direction
Future support assistant name: Sage.

Sage should be positioned as a calm, smart support guide that helps clients with common website, email, WordPress, and hosting questions, then escalates to real human support when needed.

AI messaging:
"Meet Sage, your smart support guide. Get quick help anytime, with real humans when needed."

AI principle:
AI assists. Humans remain accountable.

## Footer Brand Statement
Hippie Host delivers managed website and email hosting with real support, clean pricing, and good vibes.
